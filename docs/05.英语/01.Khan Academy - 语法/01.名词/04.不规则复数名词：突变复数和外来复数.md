## Irregular Plural Nouns：Mutant Plurals

Because their vowel sound change another vowel sound.

| word | Singular | Plural |
| ---- | -------- | ------ |
| 1    | foot     | feet   |
| 2    | woman    | women  |
| 3    | man      | men    |
| 4    | tooth    | teeth  |
| 5    | goose    | geese  |
| 6    | mouse    | mice   |
| 7    | louse    | lice   |
